What is Word-Amble?

Word-Amble offers an experience akin to Scrabble, inviting users to skillfully extract words from a given string of 10 characters in order to earn points!

Inspirations

The Inspiration for this puzzle draws from the New York Times Spelling Bee and an array of other engaging games. Our decision to infuse it with a retro theme was sparked by nostalgia for vintage computers, ASCII art, and classic games such as Fallout.
