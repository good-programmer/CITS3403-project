What is Word-Amble?

Word-Amble offers an experience akin to Scrabble, inviting users to skillfully extract words from a given string of 10 characters in order to earn points!

Inspirations

This game was inspired by the NYT Spelling Bee. Our decision to infuse it with a retro theme was sparked by nostalgia for vintage computers, ASCII art, and classic games such as Fallout.
